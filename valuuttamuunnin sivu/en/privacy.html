Privacy & cookies

This page explains how cookies and possible third-party services are used on this site.

1. General

This site provides a currency conversion service and related information. Cookies may be used to ensure the technical operation of the service, improve usability and enable possible advertising.

By using this site, you accept the use of cookies in accordance with this privacy and cookie policy.

2. What are cookies?

A cookie is a small text file stored in the user's browser. Cookies do not contain personal data as such and cannot be used to directly identify an individual.

Cookies are used for example to:

  • ensure the technical functioning of the service
  • improve usability and user experience
  • produce visitor statistics and analytics
  • enable targeted advertising (if ads are shown)

3. First-party cookies

The site may use first-party cookies that are required for the normal operation and usability of the site.

4. Third-party cookies

The site may use third-party services, such as analytics and advertising networks. These services may store their own cookies in the user's browser.

For example, the site may use:

  • Google AdSense / Google Ads advertising services
  • Google Analytics (if enabled)

These services may collect information such as:

  • browser and device type
  • time spent on the site and number of visits
  • which pages the user visits

Advertising networks may also use this information to display targeted ads across different sites. The information is not linked to an identified person.

5. Google AdSense and personalised ads

If Google AdSense is used on the site, Google and its partners may use cookies to serve personalised or non-personalised ads.

Google may, for example, use the DoubleClick cookie to optimise ad display.

More information: Google advertising cookies

6. Managing cookies

You can block or delete cookies in your browser settings. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality or user experience of the site.

7. Processing of personal data

The site does not collect personal data unless the user voluntarily provides such data, for example when sending feedback or making contact.

8. Changes to this policy

This privacy and cookie policy may be updated due to service development or changes in legislation.

9. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this privacy and cookie policy, you can contact the site owner. When using this in production, you can add your actual contact details here.
