About this site

Background information about this currency converter demo and the exchange rates used.

Purpose of the service

This currency converter is a simple tool for comparing and converting different currencies. The site is designed for users who want a quick and clear way to check up-to-date exchange rates directly in the browser.

The goal is to keep the interface minimal: enter an amount, select your currencies and see the result instantly. No plugins or extra software is needed.

Exchange rates

The site uses the Frankfurter API, which is based on market mid-rates. This means the rates shown here are indicative and may differ from the final buy/sell rates used by your bank or currency exchange.

The rates are updated several times per day according to the Frankfurter service. Any delays or differences in exchange rates are a result of the data source and changing market conditions.
